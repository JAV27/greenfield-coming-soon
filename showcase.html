About

Greenfield is proud to announce its inaugural TapIn Showcase! This elite event will feature six of the best teams in New England and well over 50 collegiate coaches. On the heels of a pandemic we hope to give these players a much needed opportunity to showcase their talent.


Greenfield has partnered with TapIn Mobile Solutions to provide the event technology. TapIn is a social impact football platform that powers national leagues in developing nations, and is launching in the US in 2021!


Providing opportunities to players is a core value of both Greenfield and TapIn. We are happy to run this event free of charge to give these elite athletes exposure to some of the best schools in the country.



Location



The event will be played at Baker Field at Rivers school in Weston Massachusetts.
